The Administrator Role

The Administrator serves as the primary overseer of the SPARK repository. Their core responsibility is to maintain the integrity of the platform by managing the high-level infrastructure. This includes the authentication of Faculty Guides and the mass enrollment of the student body through bulk data processing.

Beyond simple management, the Admin acts as the final authority on project data. They have the power to audit all submitted research, resolve technical discrepancies, and ensure that the digital archive remains organized and secure. Their role is essential for setting up the academic environment so that guides and students can interact seamlessly.

Key Operations:

  • Validation and registration of Departmental Guides.
  • Executing bulk student imports to ensure zero-delay access at the start of semesters.
  • System-wide monitoring of research activity and repository health.
