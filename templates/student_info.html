The Student Role

The Student is the primary driver of research and the ultimate beneficiary of the SPARK library. While their interaction with the system is "Read-Only," their role in the workflow is to produce the original intellectual property that populates the platform. Students use the system as a powerful reference tool to learn from previous academic years.

By accessing the repository, students can search for projects by department, title, or guide. This transparency allows for a collaborative learning environment where students can see the standard of work expected of them. Their responsibility is to work under their assigned Guide to ensure their own research is eventually worthy of being archived within the system for future generations.

Key Operations:

  • Utilizing the global search to find existing research papers and projects.
  • Filtering historical data to understand departmental research trends.
  • Accessing a centralized library of academic work for peer learning.
